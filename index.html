How it works

Three steps.
Zero frustration.

01 —

Describe it

Type what you need in plain English. No templates, no dropdowns — just describe the spreadsheet like you'd explain it to a colleague.

02 —

AI builds it

SheetMind reads your sheet context, generates the full structure — headers, formulas, sample rows — and writes it directly into your spreadsheet.

Start filling in

Your sheet is ready. Columns are sized, header row is frozen, formulas are live. Just start entering your real data.
