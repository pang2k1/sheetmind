Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

SheetMind does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your API key and usage data stay in your browser.

What data SheetMind handles

SheetMind handles the following data solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Your Claude API key — stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. Never transmitted to SheetMind's servers.
  • Your prompts — the text you type into the SheetMind panel is sent directly to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your sheet structure. SheetMind does not store your prompts.
  • Your Google Sheet content — SheetMind reads the first rows of your active sheet to provide context to the AI. This data is sent to the Claude API along with your prompt and is not stored by SheetMind.
  • Usage count — a counter tracking how many sheets you've generated this month is stored locally in your browser to enforce the free tier limit. This number is never sent anywhere.

What SheetMind does not do

  • Does not collect your name, email address, or any personal information
  • Does not transmit your API key or sheet data to SheetMind's servers
  • Does not track your browsing activity or behavior outside of Google Sheets
  • Does not sell or share your data with third parties
  • Does not use cookies or analytics trackers
  • Does not store your spreadsheet content anywhere

Third-party services

SheetMind interacts with two third-party services to function:

Anthropic (Claude API) — your prompts and sheet context are sent to Anthropic's API to generate the spreadsheet structure. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy. API data submitted by users is not used to train Anthropic's models by default.

Google (Sheets API) — SheetMind uses Google OAuth to access your Google Sheet. This allows the extension to read your sheet's current content and write the generated structure back. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Sheets access

SheetMind requests permission to read and write to your Google Sheets. This access is used exclusively to:

  • Read the first rows of your currently open sheet for AI context
  • Write the generated structure (headers, rows, formulas) into your sheet

SheetMind only accesses the sheet that is currently open. It does not access any other files in your Google Drive.

Data storage

All data stored by SheetMind lives exclusively in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API. This includes your Claude API key and your monthly generation count. You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension.

Children's privacy

SheetMind is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of SheetMind after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
